Our Philosophy

Security

Security best practices should not be thrown away when you move to the Cloud. They should be the same as your current premise Data Center.

Adaption

How is my organization going to adapt to the cloud? How do I properly support it? What does this mean to my Data Center?

Efficiency

Agility only comes with taking advantage of the cloud’s automation and scalability capabilities

TCO

Cost savings will come only by adapting to the “Cloud Paradigm”. Customers must must make their cloud services decisions like their traditional data center ones with ”open eyes”

Our Process

1. Review

Review the customer's network, security posture and applications.

2. Advise

Advise clients on how to determine if an application is an Appropriate candidate for a cost effective migration to the cloud.

3. Analyze

Analyze potential cost (TCO) of application migration to the cloud

5. Support
